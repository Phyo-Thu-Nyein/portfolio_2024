About

Hey, I'm Phyo Thu Nyein — a developer who got hooked on building things for the web and never really stopped. What started as curiosity turned into full-blown projects, late-night debugging sessions, and a lot of Stack Overflow tabs. These days, I also lean on AI tools to move faster and build smarter.

I passed my Matriculation Exam with 6 distinctions (2017–2018) and studied at Mandalay Technological University. Currently, I'm a Full-Stack Developer at Myanmar Information Technology (MIT), where I've been building production software since early 2025.

I got into programming right after high school — and it stuck.
